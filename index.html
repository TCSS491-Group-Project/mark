Use the following keys to move the ball around the maze. W is used for Up. S is used for Down. A is used for Left. D is used for Right. There are also golden color coins that can be used to pay for the maze path solution. To receive a path that solves the maze from your current position, press the space key. Cost of solution equals 10 coins. There is a blue thunder trap that you must avoid as you go along in finding your way out because if you fall in it, it will send you back to the beginning of the maze, and will decrease your coins by one.
